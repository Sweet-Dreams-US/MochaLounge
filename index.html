A neighborhood lounge

Slow mornings.
Long afternoons.
Coffee that makes both better.

Brian Till opened the original Mocha Lounge on Covington Road in 2004 with a simple idea — a place where Fort Wayne could slow down. Twenty years later, the espresso machine is still on the same counter, the regulars still get the corner booth, and the second location on Dupont keeps the same promise. Coffee & community.

What We Pour

Three things we
take seriously.

01

Hand-crafted drinks.

Espresso, cortados, lattes, mochas, cold brew, real-fruit smoothies, and seasonal teas. Nothing pre-mixed, nothing reheated.

02

Made-to-order kitchen.

Breakfast sandwiches, wraps, and bowls until 11. Lunch from 10 to 2 — Club Lounger, Smokey Garlic BLT, Sweet n' Spicy Chicken, Veggie Naan. Local where we can.

03

House-baked pastries.

The pecan coffee cake people drive across town for. Muffins, cookies, scones, and desserts — with gluten-free and vegan options every day.
